Story Guide

How It Works

From finding verified projects to planting trees on the ground — here's how every visit creates real-world impact.

How We Fund & Plant Trees
1

We Find the Projects

We identify verified reforestation projects through Tree-Nation across 14+ countries on six continents.

2

We Allocate Funds

Funds are strategically distributed to projects based on ecological impact, geographic diversity, and community benefit.

Our Partnership Model
3

Partners Join Us

Nonprofits and mission-driven organizations sign up and receive a Tree-Nation account linked to our forest.

4

The Badge Goes Live

Partners place a sustainability badge on their website, connecting their digital presence to real environmental action.

5

Visits Plant Trees

Website activity triggers real tree planting through our system — every page visit contributes to reforestation.

The Full Picture
6

Trees Go in the Ground

On-the-ground teams plant and care for trees at verified sites worldwide — each tree tracked and certified.

7

Full Transparency

Every tree is tracked, certified, and reported on our public dashboard — traceable from badge to ground.
