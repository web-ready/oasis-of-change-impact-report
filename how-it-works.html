Story Guide

How It Works

From identifying verified projects to tracking every tree on our public dashboard — here's the full story of how Oasis of Change plants trees and creates real-world impact.

Part 1 — How We Fund & Plant Trees
1

We Find the Projects

We identify verified reforestation projects through Tree-Nation across 14+ countries. Each project is evaluated for ecological credibility, geographic diversity, and community impact before funds are allocated.

2

We Allocate Funds

Funds are strategically distributed to projects based on ecological and community impact. Our allocation prioritises geographic diversity, long-term survival rates, and the greatest measurable benefit to local ecosystems.

3

Trees Go in the Ground

On-the-ground project partners plant and care for trees at verified sites worldwide. Every tree is tracked, verified, and issued a certificate through Tree-Nation — creating a direct link from donation to forest.

Part 2 — Our Partnership Model
4

Partners Join Us

Nonprofits and mission-driven organisations sign up to partner with Oasis of Change. Each partner receives a Tree-Nation account linked to our forest, connecting their digital presence to real-world reforestation.

5

The Badge Goes Live

Partners place a sustainability badge on their website. This small widget serves as a public commitment to environmental impact — and the trigger that connects their digital footprint to real tree planting.

6

Visits Plant Trees

Every page visit (or other configured trigger) on a partner's website contributes to tree planting through our system. Website activity becomes direct environmental action — no extra effort required from visitors.

Part 3 — The Full Picture
7

Full Transparency

Every tree is tracked, certified, and reported on our public dashboard. From badge to ground, every planting is traceable — creating a complete transparency loop that connects partner activity to verified environmental outcomes.

The Complete Loop
