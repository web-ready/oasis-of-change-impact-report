Third-Party Verification

All tree-planting certificates are independently verified and authenticated through Tree-Nation's comprehensive verification platform. These official documents provide transparent, auditable proof of our environmental impact and measurable carbon offset contributions to global reforestation efforts.

Impact Documentation Standards

Our certificate portfolio focuses on substantial reforestation projects of 50 trees or greater, representing our commitment to meaningful environmental impact. While we maintain numerous smaller-scale plantings, we prioritize documentation of significant contributions that demonstrate measurable progress toward global reforestation and carbon sequestration objectives.
