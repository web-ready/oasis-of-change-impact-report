Disclaimers & Notes

Verification notice

All certificates shown are independently verified through Tree-Nation or the stated issuing body (e.g. Eden Reforestation, Usambara Biodiversity). CO₂ offset values are estimates provided by project partners based on species and geography and are not guaranteed outcomes.

Impact documentation standards

Certificates are issued for projects of 50 trees or more where documentation is available. Smaller plantings are tracked in our impact reports but may not have a separate certificate on this page.

Fiscal year and historical data

Planting cycles are reported by fiscal year (FY). Historical plantings (e.g. pre-2024) and partner certificates are included for transparency and may use different verification or reporting standards.
