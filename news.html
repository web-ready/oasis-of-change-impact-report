Madagascar project completed

Completed

We contributed to Eden Reforestation Projects in Madagascar during the 2024-2025 FY tree planting cycle (675 trees). The project was completed during that cycle. We continued our reforestation work in new geographies for 2025-2026.

Eden completed the project during that cycle, so we were unable to allocate there again this year. Madagascar remained a priority geography for us; we redirected our 2025-2026 allocation to 14 countries across six continents.
