Methodology & notes

  1. 1 Trees recorded through Tree-Nation. Tree counts update live from the Tree-Nation API when available. See Verified Breakdown for details.
  2. 2 Planting sites include all reported locations: legacy, mixed, and verified.
  3. 3 CO₂ offset is from verified Tree-Nation certificates. Includes all Oasis-funded fiscal year projects, historical plantings, and planting partner contributions. Updated once per fiscal year. Fiscal year impact reports on the Impact page show Oasis-funded project totals only.
  4. 4 Some data comes from third-party partners and may not be current.
  5. 5 Some sites include trees planted before Oasis of Change, Inc. was incorporated (e.g. Eden Reforestation Projects, Nepal; National Forest Recovery, United States).
  6. 6 The planting sites listed here do not include partner organization sites (e.g. Stanley Park Ecology Society, Sustainable WWW). Partner tree counts are included in the Verified and Grand Total figures above; see Verified Breakdown for the full breakdown.
