Data Transparency Notice

This database combines verified species data from Tree-Nation projects with legacy data from Refoorest partner projects. While we strive for accuracy, some legacy data may require verification. Species marked as "Verified" have been confirmed through our Tree-Nation platform, while "Legacy Partner" data comes from our Refoorest partnership.
